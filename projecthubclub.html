Hubclub

2024

Brand Design

HubClub, as the name suggests, is a cybercafe where it is intended to be the central site for all activities related to video games. Here you can play video games for unlimited time, eat, drink and even belong to an active community full of people interested in the same hobby. Inspired by the nostalgia of old games and the computer world, we immersed ourselves in the universe of codes and pixels to create a visual identity that speaks directly to the hearts of gamers. Black and phosphorescent green, reminiscent of programming codes, became our protagonists. These vibrant, eye-catching colors not only capture attention, but also convey the energy and excitement of the gaming experience. The logo, with its pixelated style and simple yet distinctive typography, is HubClub's hallmark. I wanted it to be easily recognizable and memorable to everyone who shares the same passion for video games. When designing the concepts and menu, we made sure it was adaptable to all formats, from posters to digital displays. Simplicity and visual cohesion are key, allowing all elements to complement each other harmoniously. HubClub's graphic identity is a tribute to gamer culture, where retro aesthetics combine with modern technology to create a welcoming and exciting space for all video game enthusiasts.
