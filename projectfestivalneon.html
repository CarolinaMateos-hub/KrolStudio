Festival Neon

2022

Editorial Design

In this new work, I was invited to create the poster for the new edition of the Neon 2024 Festival, where several artists from the national scene gather to share their works based on this year's concept: the fusion between sound and light. For the design of this poster we focused on the abstraction that symbolizes the concept and in the center of the poster we present an abstract form. This central element, with its fluid lines and organic shapes, suggests movement and energy, evoking the synergy between the sound waves and the light installations that the artists exhibit. On the other hand, the typography chosen is modern and stylized, complementing the abstract design without detracting from its prominence. Neon colors were used to give depth and highlight key event information, such as the festival name, dates and location. The poster, with its bold design and eye-catching colors, not only acts as an advertisement for the festival, but also as a work of art in its own right. Its abstract shape and neon colors capture the imagination of viewers, inviting them to immerse themselves in a world where art, sound and light intertwine in a spectacular way.
