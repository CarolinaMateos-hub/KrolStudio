Coockclon Magazine

2022

Editorial Design

With time at my disposal, I decided to undertake the creation of a Christmas cooking magazine. For this project, I carefully selected the typical recipes of my childhood and looked for images that were appropriate to the chosen aesthetics, thus ensuring visual and thematic coherence. The main objective of a magazine of this nature is to provide the reader with a pleasant and didactic guide to cooking and enjoying food. With this premise in mind, the name of the magazine was chosen by combining the words “Cook”, which means to cook, and “Clone”, which evokes the idea of repetition. Once the name was defined, my intention was to design the magazine in a way that it would resemble commercial publications in the market. The magazine has different sections, carefully selected images, an attractive cover and even advertisements. The end result is a carefully crafted magazine, which only needs to be printed to be complete and ready for distribution.
