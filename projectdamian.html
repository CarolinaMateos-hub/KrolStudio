Damián Castagna

For the care of your horses, wellness for everybody

2023

Brand Design

Damián Castagna was born out of a passion for the care and conservation of Polo horses. From Argentina, they travel the world with headquarters in the south of Spain, Cadiz. They stand out for being a reliable brand, where the diligence with which they treat the horses and the products used on them are first class. The owner asked me to develop their graphic identity, wanting it to adapt to different surfaces used in their daily life. Considering his main activity as a farrier and then, later on, maintenance and transport of the animals, it was clear to me that the brand needed to have a strong impact. Evoking a relationship between the strength of the work and the heat of the field, without losing the elegance of the sport was the main objective. I decided to rely on the Fraunces typeface, inspired by early 20th century typefaces such as Windsor, Souvenir and Cooper, Fraunces plays with a heavy ink but combines it with a very distinctive Art Nouveau influenced italic. With this union I achieve that the logo where the name is reflected has both refinement and a contemporary look. The beginning of the name will be accompanied by the logotype, where we can see a D for Damián Castagno, a horseshoe and the stick of the Polo sport. With this combination we wanted to give fluidity to the image so that it would not be visually heavy and he could play with it when placing this badge in other places.
