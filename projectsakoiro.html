Sakoiro

2023

Brand Design

Sakoiro is an innovative brand that fuses the wine tradition of Japan and Spain, creating a unique experience for wine lovers. This union of two worlds demands a graphic design that reflects the rich heritage and modernity of both countries. The first step in developing the graphic design for Sakoiro was to conduct extensive research on the traditions of these two countries. Traditional wine labels from both regions were studied, as well as contemporary trends in wine label design. In addition, distinctive cultural and aesthetic elements of both countries were explored. With the research as a base, the central concept of the design was defined: the fusion of the traditional and the modern, the Japanese and the Spanish. This concept was translated into a design that had to be elegant, sophisticated and at the same time innovative. The label had to communicate quality, history and the uniqueness of the Sakoiro brand. The Sakoiro logo was chosen to include only the name without any type of icon. An elegant and contemporary typography was chosen, with stylized characters reminiscent of Japanese calligraphy but with a modern touch. The label decided to incorporate traditional Japanese imagery such as waves, lakes, and characteristic trees. These graphic elements were integrated harmoniously, creating a visually appealing and meaningful scene. The creative process included several stages of sketching and revision. Initially, several design concepts were presented and refined and adjustments were made based on feedback until a final design was reached that perfectly encapsulated the brand identity.
