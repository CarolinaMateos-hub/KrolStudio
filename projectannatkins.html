Anna Atkins

2024

Editorial Design

For the exhibition dedicated to Anna Atkins, a pioneer of botanical photography, the graphic design had to pay homage to her legacy and distinctive style. The choice of colors and visual elements was carefully selected to reflect her iconic work. The graphic design focuses on a blue and white color palette, inspired by the cyanotype technique popularized by Anna Atkins. This printing method, which produces images in shades of blue, was central to her work and became her hallmark.

Krolstudio is a design studio based in Spain.

Composed by one person, Carolina Mateos is interested in working in different fields such as web design, photography or graphic design. Having a great experience in different environments, we manage to bring a unique vision to all the projects we work on.

Whether you have a question, want to collaborate on a project or commission a job, KrolStudio is at your disposal. We are here to help by offering branding, web design, editorial and photography services. Do not hesitate to contact us.

As the main image, one of Anna Atkins' original botanical photographs was selected, providing a visually appealing focal point for the exhibit. The typography used is elegant and simple, allowing the main image to be the center of attention. A serif typeface was chosen to evoke a sense of history and tradition, appropriate for a historical figure such as Atkins.
