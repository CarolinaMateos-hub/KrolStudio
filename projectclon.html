Clon

2022

Editorial Design

The cover of the album titled “CLON” had to reflect a futuristic and experimental aesthetic, in keeping with the innovative musical content of the album. To achieve this goal, several contemporary design elements were integrated to capture the essence of the title. The cover composition is balanced, with the central title dominating the space and the visuals complementing without overwhelming. The background features subtle textures that provide an abstract context, using a gradient color palette, which flows smoothly from cool to warm tones. This allows the warped background title to be the focal point and creates a sense of distortion and mutability.
